## Getting to Level 15 fast

- You want to get to level 15 fast because it unlocks travelling, which is the fastest way to earn $$$
- Fastest way is to train in gym until at 400 total stats
- After that use [Baldr's Levelling Targets](https://oran.pw/baldrstargets/) to attack weak, high level players to gain XP fast
